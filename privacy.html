Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Bazatro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Bazatro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Bazatro is designed to help users manage budgets and personal financial information. Information entered into the app may include, for example:

  • budget names and categories
  • income and expense entries
  • user-created profiles
  • optional export data generated by the user

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Financial and app-related data is primarily stored locally on your device unless and until you choose to export it. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Exports

Bazatro may allow you to export data to formats such as PDF, Excel, or image files. Any exported file is created at your request. Once exported, you are responsible for how that file is stored, shared, or protected on your device or through third-party services.

4. Voice Features

If voice-related features are available in the app, the information processed through those features is used only to provide the requested functionality. Depending on the implementation on your device, voice processing may involve Apple or other platform services.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

Bazatro may offer optional auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock premium features. Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

6. Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use limited technical information, crash reports, or diagnostic data to improve app stability, performance, and user experience. This information is used for maintenance and improvement purposes.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may rely on service providers or platform tools necessary to operate app features, process subscriptions, or improve the app.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it, remove the app, or export and manage it yourself. Exported documents remain under your control.

9. Children's Privacy

Bazatro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time, delete the app, or manage your subscription from your Apple ID account settings.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
