Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Bazatro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Bazatro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Bazatro is designed to help users manage budgets and personal financial information. Information entered into the app may include:

  • budget names and categories
  • income and expense entries
  • user-created profiles
  • export data generated by the user

This data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our own servers.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All financial and app-related data is stored locally on your device using internal storage mechanisms. We do not sell your personal information and we do not have direct access to the data stored on your device.

3. Exports and File Sharing

Bazatro allows you to export data to formats such as PDF, Excel, or image files. These files are generated only at your request. Once exported, you are responsible for how those files are stored, shared, or deleted on your device or through third-party services.

The app may also make certain files visible through iOS File Sharing (Finder/iTunes) if this feature is enabled on your device. No automatic transfer is performed.

4. Voice Features

Some features in Bazatro may use the microphone or speech recognition to enable voice commands. Audio data is processed only to provide the requested functionality.

Depending on your device, voice processing may be performed locally or by platform services (for example, Apple Speech). Bazatro does not record or store any voice recordings.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

Bazatro may offer optional auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock premium features. Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and verify access to premium features. RevenueCat may receive:

  • an automatically generated (anonymous) user identifier
  • purchase receipts provided by the App Store
  • the status of your subscription

This information is used only to activate premium features, verify your subscription, prevent fraud, and ensure proper service operation. RevenueCat does not receive personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.

7. Analytics and Diagnostics

Bazatro does not use advertising analytics or behavioral tracking tools. Limited technical information or crash reports may be used solely to improve app stability and performance.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. The only data shared with third-party services is that which is necessary for subscription functionality (RevenueCat).

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it, uninstall the app, or export and manage it yourself. Exported documents remain under your control.

10. Children's Privacy

Bazatro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. No Tracking

Bazatro does not perform advertising tracking and does not use the advertising identifier (IDFA). Purchase data and technical identifiers are not used for targeted advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

12. Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time, delete the app, or manage your subscription from your Apple ID account settings.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: bazatro@hotmail.com
